# Privacy Policy for TerraGuard Botanica

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

## Introduction

TerraGuard Botanica ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we may collect your email address and profile information
- **Photos**: Plant and mushroom photos you upload for identification purposes
- **User Content**: Notes, collections, and saved identifications
- **Location Data**: Optional location information for regional species recommendations (only when you grant permission)

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in the app, identification history
- **Log Data**: Error logs and app performance data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide plant and mushroom identification services using AI
- Improve identification accuracy and app functionality
- Provide regional species recommendations based on your location
- Save your identification history and collections
- Send notifications about app updates or new features (with your consent)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

- All user data is securely stored using industry-standard encryption
- Photos are processed for identification and may be temporarily stored for analysis
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access
- Data is stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

- **AI Processing**: Photos are sent to OpenAI's services for identification (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to respond to legal process
- **Service Providers**: With trusted service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

## Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

- **OpenAI**: For AI-powered plant and mushroom identification
- **Google Maps**: For location-based features (if enabled)
- **Stripe**: For payment processing (if you purchase premium features)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of optional data collection (like location services)
- Export your data
- Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@terraguard.app

## Children's Privacy

TerraGuard Botanica is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Data Retention

- Identification history is stored as long as you maintain your account
- You can delete individual identifications or your entire account at any time
- Upon account deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days

## Location Data

Location services are entirely optional. If you enable location:

- We use it only to provide regional species recommendations
- Location data is not stored permanently
- You can disable location services in your device settings at any time

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new privacy policy in the app
- Sending you an email notification (if you provided an email)
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

## Important Safety Disclaimer

TerraGuard Botanica provides educational information based on visual matching. It does NOT provide medical advice. Never consume any plant or mushroom without consulting an expert. We are not responsible for any health consequences resulting from the use of our app.
